How it works



1. A wish is created, such as "reduce infant mortality in Nigeria by 5% by the year 2015".

2. Donations are received towards the wish prize until a predetermined threshold is reached, such as "$2,000,000".

3. An auction is created for shares of the prize which the general public can purchase.

4. The auction closes and everyone receives some shares at the auction price.

5. If wish is fulfilled, every shareholder will be paid according to the shares they own.

OR, if the wish fails, the shares are canceled and the money goes back to the donors.
